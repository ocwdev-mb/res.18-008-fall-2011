**Track Description:** Herb Gross defines an eigenvector of a linear map Æ’ as a vector _x_ that is mapped into a constant multiple, _c_, of itself. The value of _c_ is called the eigenvalue (or characteristic) for the corresponding vector _x_.

**Instructor/speaker:** Prof. Herbert Gross
